This tool gives you a bird's-eye view of everything you've talked about with ChatGPT — organized by topic, searchable by keyword, and mapped across time. Use the tabs above to explore.

Topics are estimated using keyword matching and may not always be accurate. Some conversations may land in the wrong category or fall into "Other." Use this as a rough guide, not a precise analysis.
